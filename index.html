USC Stands with the LGBTQIA+

The University Student Council (USC) proudly joins the LGBTQIA+ community in celebrating Pride Month. Vibrant pride flags are displayed inside and outside the PNC building as a symbol of solidarity and support. These flags remind everyone that the university is a space where diversity, equality, and acceptance are valued.

PNC RAINBOW PRIDE

Pride Month is a reminder that everyone deserves a safe space to express themselves without fear or discrimination. The USC hopes to inspire students to embrace diversity and foster acceptance within and beyond the campus. Everyone is encouraged to join the celebration and continue advocating for a society where love and respect prevail.
